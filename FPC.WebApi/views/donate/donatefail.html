Donations

First Pentecostal Church is a 501(c)(3) charitable organization, and your donations are deductible to the full extent of the law.

We use PayPal as our secure method of online giving. Please be advised that a small percentage of your donation will be deducted by PayPal to cover processing fees. If you do not wish to use a PayPal account, you can click the "Donate with a Debit or Credit Card" button, which allows you to donate without having or creating a PayPal account.

We do ask for your mailing address so that we can send you a receipt. Submitting it is at your discretion.
